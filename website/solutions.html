Smart Agriculture Solution

Smart Agri Solution is designed to help growers improve crop health by providing in-depth information in a simple platform. This solution eliminates the hassle of manually calculating data and provides growers with detailed crop information and the opportunity to automate the whole operation and generate useful insights.

  • Efficacy of Ornavera sensors for all measurements: Climate, Soil and Plant level.
  • Temp and Humidity +/- 2%
  • Lux/PPFD: +/- 10%
  • Capture readings from up to 2 soil sensors per DCD
  • Capture readings from up to 2 dendrometers per DCD
  • Data capture and upload
  • DAD reliability
  • Low error rate of message send/receive (< 1%)
  • Broad LoRa coverage footprint: 15km radius
  • Provision to add more Analog / Digital Sensor
  • Minimal DCD maintenance
  • Ease of installation
